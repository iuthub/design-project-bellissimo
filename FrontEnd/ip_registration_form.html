Ordering Meals

The order of food in Tashkent from the restaurant Bellissimo is carried out on the following conditions:

The rapid pace of modern life often leaves no time for caring for a healthy and delicious food. During work, many prefer to snack by street fast food of questionable quality or purchased food from the store. In the evenings and weekends we do not have the strength to stand at the stove for several hours, preparing delicious and healthy dishes. In such cases, the Jumanji restaurant is ready to help, which provides the service of delivery of food to the house or office. Thanks to this, any dish from our menu, freshly prepared by our chefs, will be in front of you at the right time.
